## 🧾 Business Problem

Patient retention is critical for healthcare providers to ensure continuity of care and maintain stable revenue streams. Losing patients (churn) can lead to increased acquisition costs and gaps in treatment outcomes.

The objective of this analysis was to:
- Identify factors contributing to patient churn  
- Build a predictive model to classify at-risk patients  
- Provide actionable insights to improve retention strategies  

---

## 📊 Data Overview

This analysis used a structured dataset containing patient demographic, behavioral, and service-related features, including:

- Patient demographics  
- Service usage patterns  
- Billing and payment history  
- Engagement indicators  

Data was processed using Python (Pandas) and used to train a machine learning model for churn prediction.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 1. Engagement Levels Strongly Influence Churn
- Patients with lower engagement levels were significantly more likely to churn  
- Reduced interaction frequency was a leading indicator of attrition  

---

### 2. Billing and Payment Issues Increase Risk
- Patients with inconsistent payment patterns had higher churn probability  
- Billing friction may negatively impact patient satisfaction  

---

### 3. Specific Demographics Show Higher Churn Rates
- Certain patient segments exhibited higher churn tendencies  
- Targeted interventions may be required for these groups  

---

### 4. Predictive Modeling Identifies At-Risk Patients
- The model successfully classified high-risk patients based on key features  
- Enables proactive retention strategies  

---

## 💡 Recommendations

### ✔ Implement Proactive Engagement Strategies
- Increase communication with low-engagement patients  
- Use reminders, follow-ups, and outreach programs  

---

### ✔ Improve Billing Experience
- Simplify payment processes and reduce billing friction  
- Offer flexible payment options  

---

### ✔ Target High-Risk Segments
- Develop retention strategies tailored to high-risk patient groups  
- Personalize patient experiences  

---

### ✔ Leverage Predictive Analytics in Operations
- Integrate churn predictions into workflow systems  
- Allow teams to intervene before patient loss occurs  

---

## 📈 Business Impact

If implemented, these improvements could:

- Increase patient retention rates  
- Reduce revenue loss from patient attrition  
- Improve patient satisfaction and outcomes  
- Support proactive, data-driven decision-making

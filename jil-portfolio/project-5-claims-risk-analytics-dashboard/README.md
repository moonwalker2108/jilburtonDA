
## 🏥 Healthcare Claims & Risk Analysis Dashboard  
**SQL | Power BI | Excel | Healthcare Analytics**

Developed an interactive dashboard analyzing healthcare claims data, patient risk scores, and cost trends to uncover actionable insights for cost reduction and improved patient outcomes.

---

## 📊 Key Metrics
- **Total Paid:** $10.49M  
- **Average Claim Cost:** $2.1K  
- **Average Flare Risk Score:** 0.7  
- **Total Claims:** 5,000  
- **Avg Days Supply:** 64.4–64.8  

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features
- **KPI Cards:** Total paid, average claim cost, risk score, claim count  
- **Column Chart:** Total Paid by Month  
- **Line Chart:** Flare Risk Trends Over Time  
- **Scatter Plot:** Risk vs. Days Supply (segmented by race)

---

## 🛠 Tools & Techniques
- SQL (data extraction & aggregation)  
- Power BI (dashboard development & visualization)  
- Excel (data validation and export)

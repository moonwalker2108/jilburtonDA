
## 💰 Financial Performance Analysis (Retail)

Power BI | Financial Analytics

Analyzed retail financial data to evaluate revenue trends, profitability, and performance across business segments and products.

---

#### 🔍 Key Highlights

* Built an interactive Power BI dashboard to track revenue, cost, and profit performance
* Analyzed profit margins across segments and products
* Identified patterns in profitability and performance variability

---

#### 💡 Business Value

* Highlighted high-performing segments driving profitability
* Identified underperforming segments operating at a loss
* Provided insights to support pricing strategy and cost optimization

---

#### 🛠 Tools & Techniques

* Power BI (dashboard development and financial reporting)
* DAX (calculated measures for profit and margin analysis)
* Data Modeling (structured financial datasets for analysis)
* Data Visualization (trend analysis and KPI tracking)

---

#### 📊 Sample Data (Profit Margin by Segment & Product)

| Segment          | Product   | Profit Margin |
| ---------------- | --------- | ------------- |
| Small Business   | Amarilla  | 8.86%         |
| Midmarket        | Montana   | 28.90%        |
| Government       | Paseo     | 20.54%        |
| Enterprise       | Carretera | -6.95%        |
| Channel Partners | Velo      | 73.40%        |

---

#### 🔍 Key Insight

Channel Partners show significantly higher profit margins (~73%) compared to Enterprise segments, which operate at a loss, indicating potential pricing or cost inefficiencies.

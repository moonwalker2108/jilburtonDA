## 🧾 Business Problem

Healthcare organizations depend on an efficient revenue cycle to maintain financial stability. Delays in reimbursement, high claim costs, and inconsistent performance metrics can negatively impact cash flow and operational efficiency.

The objective of this analysis was to:
- Evaluate key revenue cycle performance indicators (KPIs)  
- Identify inefficiencies in claims processing and reimbursement  
- Highlight trends impacting financial performance  

---

## 📊 Data Overview

This analysis utilized a structured healthcare dataset containing claims and financial metrics, including:

- Total claims and reimbursement amounts  
- Average claim cost  
- Payment trends over time  
- Patient risk indicators (flare risk score)  

Data was transformed using SQL and visualized in Power BI to monitor KPI performance and uncover financial trends.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 1. Total Reimbursement Trends Show Variability
- Monthly reimbursement totals fluctuated, indicating inconsistent cash flow  
- Periods of lower reimbursement aligned with increased claim volume, suggesting processing inefficiencies  

---

### 2. Average Claim Cost Highlights Cost Drivers
- Higher average claim costs were associated with specific patient groups and treatment types  
- Variability in claim cost suggests opportunities for cost optimization  

---

### 3. Patient Risk Scores Correlate with Financial Impact
- Higher flare risk scores were associated with increased claim costs  
- High-risk patient groups contributed disproportionately to total spend  

---

### 4. KPI Monitoring Reveals Performance Gaps
- Key metrics such as total claims, total paid, and average cost revealed inconsistencies across reporting periods  
- Lack of standardization in KPI tracking may limit visibility into performance issues  

---

## 💡 Recommendations

### ✔ Improve KPI Standardization
- Establish consistent definitions and tracking methods for revenue cycle KPIs  
- Ensure alignment across departments  

---

### ✔ Optimize High-Cost Claim Segments
- Analyze high-cost patient groups to identify cost-saving opportunities  
- Implement targeted care management strategies  

---

### ✔ Enhance Financial Forecasting
- Use historical KPI trends to improve revenue projections  
- Anticipate fluctuations in reimbursement and claim volume  

---

### ✔ Implement Real-Time KPI Dashboards
- Enable continuous monitoring of financial performance  
- Support faster, data-driven decision-making  

---

## 📈 Business Impact

If implemented, these improvements could:

- Increase financial transparency across the revenue cycle  
- Improve cash flow predictability  
- Reduce unnecessary costs in high-expense areas  
- Strengthen overall operational efficiency
